# MTG App User Research Framework
**Research Validation for 3-Phase Delivery Plan**

## Research Objectives
1. **Validate Phase 1 MVP feature selection** with target users
2. **Test user interest/willingness-to-pay** for AI differentiator features
3. **Understand user workflows**: deck building vs collection management vs trading priorities
4. **Identify potential pivot points** if AI prototypes fail

## User Interview Framework

### Target Segments (15-20 interviews total)

**Segment A: Casual Players (5-7 interviews)**
- Play MTG recreationally with friends/family
- Limited tournament experience
- Modest collection size (< 500 cards)
- Budget-conscious approach

**Segment B: Competitive Players (5-7 interviews)**
- Regular tournament attendance (FNM, competitive events)
- Meta-focused deck building
- Larger collection for testing/optimization
- Performance-driven decisions

**Segment C: Collectors (3-6 interviews)**
- Focus on card value, rarity, condition
- Large collections (1000+ cards)
- Investment/speculation mindset
- Storage and organization challenges

### Core Interview Questions

**Opening & Context (5 minutes)**
1. How long have you been playing MTG?
2. How would you describe your play style? (casual/competitive/collector)
3. What MTG apps/tools do you currently use, if any?
4. What's your biggest frustration with managing your MTG experience?

**Deck Building Workflow (10 minutes)**
1. Walk me through how you build a new deck from start to finish
2. What tools do you use for deck construction and testing?
3. How do you research card synergies and meta information?
4. What would make deck building faster/easier for you?

**Collection Management (10 minutes)**
1. How large is your collection? How do you organize it?
2. How do you track what cards you own vs need?
3. Do you track card values? How often?
4. What's your biggest pain point in collection management?

**AI Features Validation (15 minutes)**

**Feature 1: Binder-Page Bulk Scanning**
- *"Imagine you could photograph 9-18 cards at once and have them automatically identified and added to your digital collection. How valuable would this be to you?"*
- Follow-up: How much time do you spend manually cataloging cards?
- Willingness to pay: What's this feature worth to you monthly?

**Feature 2: Physical Storage Tracking**
- *"How do you currently remember where specific cards are stored? (binders, boxes, etc.)"*
- *"Would QR codes on storage containers that link to digital inventory interest you?"*
- Pain points: How often do you search for cards you know you own?

**Feature 3: Agentic Trade Scout**
- *"If an AI could automatically scan trade platforms and alert you to profitable trades for cards you own, how useful would that be?"*
- Follow-up: Do you actively trade/sell cards? How do you find opportunities?
- Privacy concerns: What data would you be willing to share?

**Phase Prioritization (10 minutes)**
1. **Phase 1 Features**: Deck builder, collection manager, basic card database
   - Which would you use first? Most often?
   - What's missing from this foundation?

2. **Phase 2 Features**: Advanced analytics, meta tracking, tournament preparation tools
   - Which appeals most to your play style?
   - How much would advanced features be worth?

3. **Phase 3 Features**: Social features, marketplace integration, content creation tools
   - Would social features make you switch apps?
   - How important is community vs personal tools?

**Competitive Landscape (5 minutes)**
1. What do you love/hate about your current MTG apps?
2. What would make you switch from your current solution?
3. Have you tried: Moxfield, ManaBox, Archidekt, Dragon Shield, Delver Lens?

**Closing (5 minutes)**
1. If this app existed today, what would be your first use case?
2. What's the maximum you'd pay monthly for a comprehensive MTG app?
3. Any other features or pain points we didn't discuss?

### Interview Success Metrics

**Quantitative Validation Targets:**
- 70%+ find bulk scanning "very valuable" or "extremely valuable"
- 60%+ willing to pay $3-10/month for AI features
- Clear workflow priority ranking across segments
- Competitive switching threshold identification

**Qualitative Insights:**
- Feature interaction patterns and user journeys
- Pain points that existing apps don't address
- Pricing sensitivity ranges by segment
- Technical feasibility concerns from users

### Follow-up Survey Distribution

**Post-interview Survey (5-10 minutes)**
- Quantify findings from interviews
- Validate pricing models
- Feature prioritization ranking
- Demographic segmentation
- Current app satisfaction scores

### Recruitment Strategy

**Channels:**
- Local MTG stores (3-4 locations)
- MTG Reddit communities (/r/magicTCG, /r/EDH)
- Discord servers (competitive and casual)
- Social media MTG groups
- Tournament organizers for competitive players

**Incentives:**
- $25 Amazon gift card per 45-minute interview
- Early access beta invitation
- Exclusive MTG app features preview

This framework will generate the data needed to validate our 3-phase delivery plan and make data-driven decisions about feature prioritization and AI prototype development.
